01 Privacy Policy

Shadow Profile is operated by Venux Studio. This policy explains what information we handle when you use this service, why, and what we do with it. The short version: we handle as little as possible, we keep nothing, and we don't share anything with anyone except where strictly necessary to deliver the scan result.

02 What we collect

Email addresses

When you enter an email address to run a scan, that address is transmitted over an encrypted HTTPS connection to the HaveIBeenPwned (HIBP) API to check it against their breach database. We do not store your email address at any point in this process. It is not written to any database, log file, or analytics system on our end.

Phone numbers

If you use the phone number check feature (available in the ₹99 plan), your number is hashed locally in your browser using SHA-256 before being checked against our dataset. The plaintext number never leaves your device. Only the hash is used for lookup. No number is stored.

Payment information

Payments are processed entirely by Razorpay. We do not handle, transmit, or store any card numbers, UPI IDs, or banking credentials. When you pay, you interact directly with Razorpay's secure payment interface. See the Payments section for details.

Technical data

Like all web services, our hosting infrastructure (Vercel) may log standard request metadata — IP addresses, browser type, timestamp — as part of normal server operation. These logs are not used for analytics, tracking, or profiling and are subject to Vercel's own data retention policies. We do not access or use these logs to identify individual users.

Bottom line on data collection We do not build profiles. We do not track users across sessions. We do not know who you are, and we have no interest in finding out. The scan is designed to be as stateless as possible.

03 What we never do

  • Store email addresses or scan results after a query is complete
  • Sell, rent, or share user data with third parties for commercial purposes
  • Send marketing emails or newsletters (we have no email list)
  • Use retargeting pixels or advertising networks on this site
  • Use Google Analytics, Meta Pixel, or similar behavioural tracking tools
  • Share data with data brokers or aggregators
  • Use your information to train machine learning models
  • Contact you after your visit unless you initiated the contact

04 Payments

All payments are processed by Razorpay, a PCI-DSS compliant payment gateway. When you choose to unlock a report, you are redirected to a Razorpay-hosted payment interface. We receive only a payment confirmation — an order ID and success status — sufficient to unlock your report. We do not receive or store your card details, UPI credentials, or banking information.

Razorpay's privacy policy governs how they handle your payment data. You can review it at razorpay.com/privacy.

Your unlocked report is delivered immediately in your browser session. Because no account is created and no data is stored, we cannot recover a report after your session ends. This is why we auto-generate a PDF for download — please save it before closing the tab.

05 Cookies

We use no tracking cookies. The only browser storage used on this site is session-level memory within your current tab — for example, holding your scan result while you're viewing it. This data is not persisted across sessions and is never transmitted to any server.

Razorpay may set cookies within its own payment interface as required for fraud prevention. These are subject to Razorpay's cookie policy and operate within their iframe only.

06 Third-party services we use

  • HaveIBeenPwned API — receives your email address to perform the breach lookup. Their privacy policy applies to this interaction. HIBP does not store queries. haveibeenpwned.com/Privacy
  • Razorpay — payment processing. Receives payment data only. Does not receive your email or scan results.
  • Vercel — hosting infrastructure. Standard server logs only, not shared with us.
  • Google Fonts — Montserrat font loaded via Google's CDN. Google may log font request metadata. No user data is transmitted to Google from this service beyond the font load request.

That is the complete list. We have not integrated any analytics platforms, ad networks, social sharing SDKs, or customer data platforms.

07 Your rights & contact

Because we don't store personal data, most typical data rights requests (access, deletion, portability) are moot — there is nothing to access or delete. If you have a concern about how a specific piece of data was handled during your session, or if you believe our practice doesn't match what's described here, please contact us.

If you're an Indian resident, the Information Technology Act, 2000 and related rules govern certain aspects of data processing. We comply with applicable Indian data protection requirements.

About checking others' data The service is designed for self-checks. Checking someone else's email or phone number without their consent may violate their privacy and is against these terms. If you want to share the tool with someone, send them the link — don't run the scan on their behalf.

10 Reports & results

Scan results are provided for informational purposes only. The breach data we surface comes from publicly documented, third-party sources — primarily the HaveIBeenPwned database. We present this data in good faith but cannot guarantee its completeness, accuracy, or currency.

A clean result does not mean you have not been breached. Many breaches are never publicly disclosed, take years to be identified, or haven't been indexed by available databases. You should always practise good digital hygiene regardless of your scan result.

Paid reports — including the recovery checklist and data broker removal guide — are provided as practical guidance only. They do not constitute legal, financial, or cybersecurity advice. We recommend seeking professional advice for complex situations involving identity fraud or financial compromise.

Because reports are generated in your browser session and we store nothing server-side, we cannot re-issue a report after your session ends. Please save your PDF immediately after purchase.
