Common questions
I paid but haven't received anything. What should I do?
+

Your paid report unlocks instantly in the same browser session after payment. If you've closed the tab, email us at venuxstudiox@gmail.com with your payment reference and we'll sort it out promptly.

Is my email address safe when I use Shadow Profile?
+

Yes. We never store, log, or share your email address. It's used only to query the XposedOrNot breach database in real time and is discarded immediately after. No accounts, no tracking.

Can I request removal of my data from breach records?
+

Shadow Profile doesn't host breach data — we query the XposedOrNot API. To request removal from the underlying breach database, contact XposedOrNot directly. Our paid reports also include data broker opt-out links for 15–20 removal portals.

Do you offer partnerships, white-labelling, or API access?
+

Yes — Venux Studio is open to partnerships, brand collaborations, and integration enquiries. Use the form above or email venuxstudiox@gmail.com with "Partnership" as the subject.

My scan shows no breaches but I'm sure my data was leaked. Why?
+

Our database covers thousands of publicly documented breaches. Many breaches are never disclosed, or take years to surface. A clean result means your email hasn't appeared in any breach we have on record — not that you're completely safe. Stay vigilant and use strong unique passwords.
